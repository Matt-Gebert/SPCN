Healing Service

10:00am Thursdays

Our mission is to bring healing and restoration in body, mind and spirit. As healing was a large part of Jesus' ministry as well as the early apostles we believe in God's ability to make these things possible.
The Healing Service runs at 10.00am Thursdays (February to November), and offers restoration through praise and worship, teaching, the laying on of hands and intercessory prayer.
Our vicar, Rev. Howard Langmead, along with guest speakers during the year, will preach on healing and related topics.
We have a heavy focus on Prayer Ministry, which is offered to any who attend our services.
