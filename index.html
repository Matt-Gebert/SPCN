Healing Service

10am Thursdays

Our mission is to bring healing and restoration in body, mind and spirit through the power of the Cross, by the laying on of hands and the prayer of faith.
The Healing Service 10.00am Thursday (February to November) offers restoration through praise and worship, teaching, the laying on of hands and intercessory prayer.
Our vicar, Rev. Howard Langmead, along with guest speakers during the year, will preach on healing and related topics.
The healing ministry is enhanced and supplemented by Prayer Ministry, which is offered to those who regularly attend our services.
